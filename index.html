Athan On Time

Accurate prayer times based on your current location - anywhere in the world. Finally an athan clock that's plugged in so you never run out of batteries. Hear the call to prayer loud and clear.

No Adjustment

AthanIO uses GPS to calculate your prayer time based on your location. Any city, all the time.

Unlike traditional athan clocks, AthanIO does not require you to adjust your location or prayer times. It does it for you. AthanIO uses GPS to determine your location and calculates prayers times based on your chosen calculation method.

With its beautiful digital display, AthanIO goes beyond the typical athan clock. It is also an Islamic hijri calendar and has a built in Qibla compass.

  • Displays time left until next salah
  • Access weekly and monthly prayer times
  • Choose from several athan recitations
  • Set reminders to pray
  • Select from different prayer calculation methods

No Batteries

AthanIO is always plugged in so the Athan is nice and clear. Don't worry, the charger is included.

Tired of changing the batteries on your athan clock? Hate that the athan gets quieter as the batteries in your athan clock get weaker?

AthanIO is your answer. Now your family can hear the athan from anywhere in the house, all the time.

  • MicroUSB adaptor included
  • Connects using WiFi
  • Durable and anti-scratch shell
